## VERSION 1 — INTERNAL FOUNDING CHARTER
*For the three founders. The document you return to when decisions get hard.*

---

### who we are

three people. almost forty years of friendship. one from california. one from arizona. one from seattle. we've watched each other grow up, grow older, and grow into something we couldn't have named when we were younger — a shared belief that there is more to this than what we've been told.

we met in sedona in the spring of 2025. we stood at bradshaw ranch. we traced petroglyphs at palatki that hands carved into red rock a thousand years ago. we looked at those spiral marks and felt something we didn't need to explain to each other. the pattern was already there. we were just finally seeing it.

that's what this is. that's what we're building.

---

### why we exist

the signal is already out there.

it's in the research being done at the edges of credible science. it's in the experiences people have and then don't know how to talk about. it's in the synchronicities that keep showing up — the ones you notice but file away because there's nowhere to put them. it's in the conversations happening between people who are quietly, seriously asking the biggest questions a human being can ask.

but it's buried. buried under noise. buried under fear-based content designed to keep you scrolling. buried under channels that rehash the same events with slightly different thumbnails. buried under monetization and upsell and the machinery of attention economics.

we're not interested in any of that.

we believe the medium of all of this — consciousness, synchronicity, contact, healing, evolution — is energy. not metaphorically. literally. every sense we have is a transducer of energy. every instrument that has ever measured something real — ekg, eeg, mri, telescope — is measuring energy in some form. the phenomena that this community cares about most are energy phenomena. and energy doesn't lie.

we're here to clear enough noise that the signal comes through.

---

### what we believe

**we believe humanity is in the middle of something.** not at the beginning. not at the end. in the middle of a shift in collective awareness that has been building for longer than any of us has been alive — and that is accelerating now in ways that are visible if you know where to look.

**we believe the pattern already exists in the sky.** the spiral was always there in the stars. we're just clearing enough noise to see it. that's the metaphor we chose, and we mean it literally: the signal is not something we create. it's something we reveal.

**we believe in radical positivity.** not the kind that denies hard things. the kind that chooses expansion over contraction, connection over isolation, curiosity over fear. there's enough fear in this space. we're not adding to it.

**we believe in the community.** not as an audience. not as a user base. as a collective of people who are doing the actual work of waking up — in their own lives, in their own ways, on their own timelines. our job is to hold the space and keep it clean.

**we believe energy is the through-line.** it connects every topic we care about — uap phenomena, consciousness research, synchronicity, manifestation, collective evolution, healing. these aren't separate subjects. they're different faces of the same thing.

---

### what we will never do

- monetize the community's trust
- amplify fear, division, or unfalsifiable claims
- chase engagement at the expense of signal quality
- take shortcuts on curation because the volume is high
- build walls around content that should be free
- lose sight of why we started this

---

### the founding principles

**free. always.** no tiers. no paywalls. no premium content. the signal is for everyone or it's for no one.

**transparent. completely.** we publish our curation criteria. we explain our scoring. we name our sources. we don't hide the machine.

**positive. deliberately.** positivity is not naivety. it is a choice we make every single day about what we amplify and what we let pass.

**grounded. credibly.** we lean on evidence, experience, and credible voices. we don't require proof for everything — this space doesn't work that way — but we require intellectual honesty. always.

**human. warmly.** this community is made of people who have often felt alone in what they notice and believe. we take that seriously. nobody should feel alone here.

---

### our commitment to each other

we started this as friends. we will finish it as friends — whatever finishing looks like.

decisions get made together. no founder has more weight than another. when we disagree, we return to the principles above and ask which choice is more aligned with them.

we protect the community before we protect the project. if clear the signal ever becomes something that doesn't serve the people in it, we shut it down or hand it off. the mission is not the brand. the mission is the mission.

we remember sedona. we remember the spiral in the rock. we remember that the pattern was already there.

---

### extended about section

#### why we built this

three friends. almost forty years. one conversation in sedona that became something we couldn't walk away from.

we'd all been following this space for years — the research, the voices, the events, the experiences. we believed in it. we still do. but we kept running into the same problem: for every piece of content that genuinely moved the conversation forward, there were twenty more that recycled the same events, leaned into fear, or existed primarily to sell you something.

the signal was there. it was just buried.

so we built a filter. and then we built a home for the people who wanted to find what the filter was surfacing.

that's clear the signal.

---

#### what we do

we watch the full landscape of content across consciousness research, uap phenomena, synchronicity, manifestation, healing, and collective evolution. we score it. we filter it. we surface what's actually moving the conversation forward — new information, credible voices, genuine experience — and we let everything else pass.

the scoring is transparent. the criteria are published. the curation is human, not algorithmic.

what comes through is signal.

---

#### what we believe

we believe energy is the medium of all of this. every sense you have is a transducer of energy. every instrument that has ever measured something real is measuring energy in some form. the phenomena this community cares about most are energy phenomena. and energy doesn't lie.

we believe the pattern already exists. the spiral was always in the stars. we're not inventing a new conversation — we're clearing enough noise to see the one that's been there all along.

we believe radical positivity is not naivety. it's a choice. there is enough fear in this space. we are not adding to it. every piece of content we surface, every conversation we host, every voice we amplify is chosen because it expands rather than contracts. because it connects rather than isolates. because it opens rather than closes.

we believe you're not alone in this. whatever you've noticed. whatever you've experienced. whatever question won't leave you alone. there is a community of people having the same conversation — carefully, seriously, with open minds and grounded hearts. this is where they are.

---

#### what we will never do

clear the signal will always be free. no paywalls. no premium tiers. no content locked behind a subscription. the signal is for everyone or it's for no one.

we will never amplify fear, unfalsifiable claims, or content designed to divide. we will never monetize your trust. we will never optimize for engagement over truth.

if something doesn't belong here, it doesn't get in. that's the whole job.

---

#### the invitation

you don't have to explain yourself here.

you don't have to qualify your experiences or defend your curiosity. you don't have to pretend you haven't noticed what you've noticed. you don't have to be certain — about any of it.

you just have to be willing to look.

clear the signal. find your frequency.
